Easily manage email subscriptions with a stylish form in WordPress.

== Description ==

Mailino is a user-friendly plugin that allows you to collect email subscriptions for your newsletter and grow your audience. After activating the plugin, you can customize the subscription form’s colors and styles from the settings menu.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `mailino.zip` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Extract the zip file.
3. Activate Mailino through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What email providers does Mailino support? =

Mailino currently supports emails from trusted providers like Gmail, Yahoo, and Outlook.

= How can I customize the subscription form? =

After activation, navigate to **Mailino** in your WordPress dashboard to configure the form’s main color, border radius, and size.
